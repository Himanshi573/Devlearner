American Bandogge
The American Bandogge, a cross between the ancient Mastiff and the industrious American Bulldog, has recently gained a growing number of devoted fans among active dog owners. This giant breed boasts a robust frame and an imposing presence.
Came Corso
The Cane Corso[a] is an Italian breed of mastiff. It is usually kept as a companion dog or guard dog; it may also be used to protect livestock. In the past it was used for hunting large game, and also to herd cattle.
